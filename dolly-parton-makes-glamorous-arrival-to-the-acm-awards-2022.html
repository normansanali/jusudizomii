Dolly Parton Makes Glamorous Arrival To The ACM Awards 2022

Dolly Parton has arrived!

The 76-year-old country queen made a glamorous arrival at the 2022 Academy of Country Music Awards on Monday (March 7) at Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas, Nev.

Dolly is the host of tonight’s award ceremony, which highlights and honors the best in country music.

She’ll also be performing at the show with Kelsea Ballerini, who she posed with on the blue carpet.

“I’m gonna be doing a number in the show with Kelsea Ballerini, we’re gonna be doing one of the songs from my Run, Rose, Run album,” Dolly teased in a recent interview.

Dolly also has a new book of the same name, which she co-wrote with James Patterson, out now!
