Who Is Crystal Lee? Her Bio, Age, Job

Bling Empire star Jessey Lee is the husband/baby father of Cherie Chan. According to Cherie, the two first met each other half a year after Jessey’s divorce from his ex-wife Crystal Lee. However, Crystal has a different story to tell.

Get to know her better as you scroll down.

Meet Bling Empire Star Jessey Lee Ex-Wife, Crystal Lee

Crystal Lee and the Bling Empire star Jessey Lee were married on June 18, 2011. Sadly, nearly four years later, Jessey then filed a divorce petition, and Crystal filed a counterpetition shortly thereafter. 

Reportedly, their case proceeding commenced on May 31, 2016, and one of the main reasons for the dispute was the division of their marital property. Fast forward to 3 months of hearing, the court finally made a decision and awarded Crysta $600, 789 as “part of the division of community property between” them.

The two share two daughters named Jazlyn Lee (birthday: November 9) and Joella Lee.

Now, coming to the timeline, when Jessey and Cherie Chan first met. According to  Cherie, it was half a year after Jessey’s divorce from his ex-wife. However, Crystal tagged Cherie in a comment over an IG post from 2015 mentioning “Here’s when she was trying to befriend me when she was really trying to hook up with my husband.”

This means Crystal and Cherie have connected in the past.

Even Bling Empire season 2 newcomer, Leah Qin revealed that she first met Jessey and Cherie at an exotic car event when Jessey was married to Crystal.

This revelation made fans wonder if it was the reason why Jessey took so long to marry Cherie as he might be possibly still legally married to his ex-wife. Also, the husband/wife’s abrupt leave from the show in 2022 because they wanted to “focus on their family and businesses,” seemed a little suspicious.

However,  we don’t know whose story is true here.

Talking a little about Jessey and Cherie, the two got married in Château de Saran, France in 2021, sometime after their engagement in Paris. They share a son and a daughter named Jadore and Jevon.

What Is Crystal Lee Bling Empire Star Jessey Lee Ex-Wife’s Full Name?

Crystal Lee’s full name is Crystal Ling Hoang Lee.

She hails from Dallas, Texas. Besides her hometown, she’s also resided in places including Ibiza, Spain; Paris, France; Manhattan Beach, California; and as of 2022, she lived in Beverly Hills, California.

Crystal Lee Age

Crystal Lee was born on March 8, 1988. That made her 43 years of age when Bling Empire season 2 premiered in 2022.

As per her birthday, Crystal is of the Pisces zodiac.

Crystal Lee Job

Crystal Lee is on a journey of “empowering women to create kinder humans for our world” and she does this with the help of her company VOTUMVest (which she founded in Jan 2021). Alongside advisor Pamela Day, Crystal worked as the CEO of the company, helping women to achieve financial independence by simply investing in companies that benefited them.

Besides VOTUMVest, Crystal also worked as the CEO of JOELLAJOELLA, Senior Executive Producer of Unicorn Venture Capital, and Chef Marking Officer (also the owner of LF Enterprises, as of 2022.

Prior to this, Crystal took upon the job of a producer at HDNetHDNet (Jan 2007 – Jan 2010), The Richards Group (Jan 2004 – Jan 2007), and NBCUniversal, Inc (May 2001 – Dec 2003).

Also, she’s been a fitness instructor at her alma mater Southern Methodist University from Jan 2003 to Jan 2005. (Later, she received her master’s degree in international business from the Fashion Institute of Technology in 2016.)

On top of her multiple jobs, Crystal is also a model/actor who was signed by the CESD talent agency in Jan 2010, and Kim Dawson Agency Inc in Jan 2009. So far, she’s appeared in movies and TV shows including We Are Angels (2014), Holla II (2013), The Slutty Years (2012), Blood Effects (2011), The Deep End (2010), and a few more.

Crystal stands tall at a height of 5 feet 7 inches (170 cm) and weighs 49 kg (108 lbs). Her body measurements were — waist: 24″, hips: 32″, cup: B”, shoe: 7.

When Crystal isn’t working, she likes to travel, write, and eat chocolates.

Fun Fact: Crystal speaks four languages —  English, Vietnamese, French, and Spanish. Also, she was learning Mandarin.

Who Is Crystal Lee Husband Now?

Crystal Lee is likely single now. However, after splitting with her husband Bling Empire star Jessey Lee, she went on to date Alexander Sienkiewicz. The two were last spotted together back in Dec 2016 during their trip to Cuba.

According to Crystal, she “boo” Alexander was supportive — so much that he showed up with a giant cookie, moments before their fitness workout (she joked).

Her boyfriend, Alexander is a California Polytechnic State University graduate who went on to become the chief marketing officer of “Spiraledge.com.”
