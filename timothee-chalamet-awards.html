Timothe Chalamet Awards

Timothée Chalamet Awards – Timothée Hal Chalamet is an American and French actor. He has received nominations for prestigious awards, including the Academy Award, Golden Globe Awards, and BAFTA Film Awards.

Chalamet began his acting career as a teenager, appearing in TV shows such as Homeland in 2012. He made his film debut in the comedy-drama Men, Women & Children in 2014 and gained recognition for his role in Christopher Nolan’s Interstellar.

However, his breakthrough came with the lead role in Luca Guadagnino’s Call Me by Your Name in 2017, which earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor. He has also appeared in Greta Gerwig’s films Lady Bird and Little Women, as well as taking on notable roles in Beautiful Boy, Dune, and Bones and All, which he also produced.

In addition to his acting career, Timothée Chalamet has received attention as a sex symbol and fashion icon. He is fluent in both English and French and holds dual citizenship in the United States and France. Chalamet spent summers in a small French village during his upbringing, leading to cross-cultural identity experiences.

He attended various schools, including Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, where he developed a passion for acting.

After high school, Timothée Chalamet briefly attended Columbia University before transferring to New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study to focus on his acting career. He keeps a residence in both New York and California.

While Chalamet’s personal life attracts media attention, he tends to keep his romantic relationships private. From 2018 to 2020, he was in a relationship with Lily-Rose Depp.

Outside of acting, Timothée Chalamet is an enthusiastic sports fan, particularly supporting the New York Knicks and the French football team AS Saint-Étienne. He is also a fan of hip-hop music, with rapper Kid Cudi being his biggest career inspiration.

Timothée Chalamet Awards

Chalamet’s standout performance in Call Me by Your Name earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor. He also received nominations for a BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award, and a Critics’ Choice Movie Award, all in the category of Best Actor.

Additionally, his supporting role in Lady Bird garnered attention, resulting in nominations for the Critics’ Choice Movie Award for Best Acting Ensemble and the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture, alongside the rest of the cast. Furthermore, his portrayal in Beautiful Boy earned him nominations for a Golden Globe Award, a BAFTA Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award, and a Critics’ Choice Award, all for Best Supporting Actor.
