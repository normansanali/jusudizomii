Three box sets, an EP, a soundtrack album, 15 compilation albums, and four live albums are among their collaborations. After the couple split up in 1965, Simon produced his first solo studio album, “The Paul Simon Songbook,” in 1965.

In 1972, he published his self-titled album, which was followed by albums like There Goes Rhymin’ Simon, one-trick pony, still insane after all these years, hearts and bones, and so on.
