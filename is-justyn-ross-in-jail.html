What are Cookies?

Cookies are digital treats that improve your online experience. They are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. These files contain information that helps the website remember your preferences and provide you with personalized content. Cookies are widely used across the internet to enhance user experiences and make browsing more seamless.

Types of Cookies

There are two main types of cookies: session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to remember your preferences and settings during your current browsing session. On the other hand, persistent cookies remain on your device for a longer period. They store information that helps us recognize you when you visit our site again, allowing for a more personalized experience.
