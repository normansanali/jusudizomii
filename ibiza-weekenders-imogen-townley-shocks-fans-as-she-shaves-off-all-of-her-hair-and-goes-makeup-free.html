Ibiza Weekender's Imogen Townley shocks fans as she shaves off all of her hair and goes makeup-free

Imogen Townley has shocked fans after shaving off all of her hair and going makeup free for a more natural look. 

The Ibiza Weekender star, 28, took to Instagram on Monday to share her new look with her fans saying she no longer hides behind a ‘mask’. 

‘Everyday I get to look at my beautiful bald head and smile and remember all that I have been and all I have become,’ the reality star told her followers. 

‘My beautiful bald head’: Imogen Townley shocked fans by revealing a more natural look after shaving off all of her hair and going makeup free on Instagram on Monday

Before her transformation, Imogen used to have long chestnut locks and was rarely seen without a full face of glamorous makeup. 

The former Ibiza Weekender star looked a far cry from her on screen appearance as she took to social media to explain her new perspective on beauty to her fans. 

She explained: ‘Bare to be beautiful. As I continue to shed the parts of myself that do not define who I am the closer I find myself exploring what true love and acceptance is.

‘I’ve found these last few years has just been steps of peeling back the layers of an identity built on pressure, public opinion & conditioning of the distorted society we live in.’

Before: Imogen used to have long chestnut locks and was rarely seen without a full face of glamorous makeup while appearing on Ibiza Weekender 

Imogen continued: ‘They say that the hair holds memories and for me these memories and old energies of believing I am unworthy, unattractive and unloveable are no longer truth of the person I have become. 

‘I am a Devine creation! Complete and whole. And as I find myself being called back home to my heart I new it was time to let these old beliefs and attachments go! To shed it all and come into complete bareness so I could truly see all of me and all of my beauty in all its raw uniqueness.

‘Letting go of my hair has been so liberating & empowering and given me a sense of total freedom that I have never felt before. 

‘Letting go of my hair has been so liberating & empowering and given me a sense of total freedom that I have never felt before,’ she told her followers 

‘Of course there been some moments of discomfort but all of these emotions have been completely welcomed as I meet those still conditioned parts of myself with love and gentleness, holding myself in this vulnerability and guiding myself back to truth. 

‘Everyday I get to look at my beautiful bald head and smile and remember all that I have been and all I have become. I am beauty with no edges, beauty that no longer hides behind the mask. Just true beautiful perfection,’ she signed off.  

Imogen starred on Ibiza Weekender for six seasons, from 2013 to 2017.
