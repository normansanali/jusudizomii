Cully Pratt-Solano County's Seargent Sheriff is Chris Pratt's older brother!

Cully Pratt is a local hero, a former veteran army, and an accomplished artist. Currently, he serves as the Seargent-Sheriff of the Solano County Sheriff's Office. Further, you might also know him as the older brother of actor, Chris Pratt.

Cully was born as Daniel Pratt on October 28, 1976, in Virginia, Minnesota, the United States. Apart from Chris, he has a sister named, Angie Pratt. They were born to parents, Daniel Clifton Pratt (father) and Kathleen Louise Indahl (mother).

The former veteran enlisted in the army right after high school graduation in 1995. He earned a Bachelor's degree in Business Management in 2015. His mother worked at a Safeway supermarket, while his father worked in mining and later remodeling houses. Unfortunately, Daniel died in 2014 from multiple sclerosis.

Served Eight Years in the Army

Cully served in the Army from 1995 until 2003. Initially, he wanted to be a marine but ended up in the Army infantry. During those eight years, Pratt went from active duty to a Military Police Officer, and a Recruiting and Retention Officer.

Pratt credits his military career for becoming the man he is today. He joined in the Army in search of doing something great for his country.

Solano County Sheriff

After leaving the Army, Cully joined the Pittsburg Police Department as an officer in 2003. Further, he served with the City of Rio Vista and later became the public information officer of the Solano County Sheriff's Office.

In August 2015, Cully began his career as a Deputy-Sheriff with Solano County. Likewise, the office promoted him as the Seargent-Sheriff on April 5, 2020.
