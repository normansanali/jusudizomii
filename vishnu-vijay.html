Vishnu – Career and Professional Life

Professionally, he is a famous TV actor. He made his acting debut with his role in Office in the year 2013. he has also worked on films like Mapla Singam in 2016, Ivan Yarendru Therikiratha in 2017, and Kalari in 2018. At present, the TV actor is portraying the lead role of Prabhu in the TV series Sathya on the network channel Zee Tamil.

Likewise, he made his impact as an actor by playing the role of Vishnuvardhan in the TV series, Office. This series also co-stars famous actors like Karthik and Shruthi Raj. The TV actor then made his debut as a film actor with the film Mapla Singam. It is a 2016 film in which he plays a supporting role. It is an action film that stars popular actors Vemal and Anjali in the lead roles.

Moreover, his 1st film as the lead actor is Ivan Yarendru Therikiratha in 2017. This movie had a delayed release. This film also failed to garner attention at the box office as a result of other high-profile releases. Ivan Yarendru Therikiratha was a comedy film about a man who was born on Valentine’s Day. In this project, Vishny worked alongside popular actresses Ishaara Nair and Varsha Bollamma.
