WWE RAW Complete Spoiler Lineup for 12/11 Episode

Tonight’s episode of WWE RAW will be broadcasting live from Rocket Mortgage Fieldhouse in Cleveland, Ohio, featuring all the latest build to the upcoming Royal Rumble Premium Live Event next month.

Sean Ross Sapp of Fightful Select provided the complete spoiler lineup for tonight’s episode of WWE RAW.

  • Jey Uso’s promotional segment.
  • Jey Uso facing Drew McIntyre in a match.
  • A vignette featuring Nakamura.
  • A backstage segment at Judgment Day.
  • The Maxxine Dupri-Ivy Nile segment.
  • Rhea Ripley taking on Maxxine Dupri in a match.
  • CM Punk delivering a promo.
  • Bronson Reed squaring off against Ivar in a match.
  • A promotional segment for Judgment Day.
  • Katana Chance & Kayden Carter facing Candice LeRae & Indi Hartwell in a tag team match.
  • Becky Lynch’s promotional segment.
  • A SPOILER TAG TEAM MATCH.
  • Cody Rhodes going up against Shinsuke Nakamura in a match.

Backstage WWE RAW Notes

  • WWE Main Event tapings will feature a match between Natalya and Elektra Lopez.
  • Von Wagner is scheduled to compete at the WWE Main Event tapings against Edris Enofe.
  • The main event referee assignment is designated for Shawn Bennett.
  • Additionally, Valhalla is listed to appear on the show.

Other WWE RAW Spoilers

  • The main event segment is scheduled to span across three segments.
  • Women’s matches are allocated one segment each, while men’s matches, excluding the main event, are allocated two segments each.
  • Ivy Nile is set to accompany Maxxine to the ring.
  • Piper Niven & Chelsea Green are booked for commentary during the women’s tag team match.
  • In a SPOILER TAG, DIY & Miz will face Imperium.
