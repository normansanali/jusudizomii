Introducing Alec Baldwin and Hilaria's Newborn Son, Eduardo Pau Lucas (photo)


Fitness entrepreneur and co-host of the “Mom Brain” podcast, Hilaria Baldwin, together with her husband Alec Baldwin, celebrated the arrival of their fifth child, a son, on Tuesday, September 8.

The couple has proudly presented their son to the world, along with his name.

Hilaria and Alec Baldwin took to their individual Instagram accounts to share a photo of their son and announced his name as Eduardo Pau Lucas.

The Spanish-born beauty and the 62-year-old Hunt For Red October actor are already parents to daughter Carmen, seven, and sons Rafael, five, Leonardo, three, and Romeo, two. 

Alec is also the father of daughter Ireland, 24, whom he shares with ex-wife Kim Basinger.
