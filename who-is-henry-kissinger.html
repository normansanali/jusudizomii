Who Is Henry Kissinger?

Henry Kissinger’s journey began as a Jewish refugee fleeing Nazi Germany in 1938. Settling in the United States, he excelled academically, graduating summa cum laude from Harvard College in 1950 under the guidance of William Yandell Elliott. Furthering his education, Kissinger earned his Master of Arts and Doctor of Philosophy at Harvard University in 1951 and 1954, respectively.
