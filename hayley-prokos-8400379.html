Hayley Prokos - InStyle

Title: Associate Fashion Commerce Editor

Education: Northwestern University

Location: Los Angeles, California

Expertise: Fashion and Style

Highlights:

  • Hayley holds a master's degree in Journalism from Northwestern University.
  • She has over five years of writing and editing experience.
  • She has written for The Zoe Report, SELF Magazine, and Ekathimerini (a NYT affiliate in Athens, Greece).

Experience

Hayley is the Associate Fashion Editor, Commerce at InStyle. She has been writing for as long as she can remember and has interviewed many designers, entrepreneurs, and experts in the areas of fashion, beauty, wellness, and travel.

Education

Hayley graduated from Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism, having spent much of her time there exploring urbanism and public mental health care in Chicago, Europe, and the Caribbean. Before that, she received her bachelor's degree in English and French from the University of California, Davis.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
