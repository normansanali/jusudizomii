Next Mondays RAW Confirmed For Staples Center In L.A.?

Credit: F4WOnline.com

KNBC out of Los Angeles, California is reporting that WWE RAW for next Monday, May 25, is now officially scheduled to be held at the Staples Center in L.A.

According to the KNBC report, WWE is scheduled to hold a press conference at 7:00PM ET to make the announcement official.

A local station out of Denver reports that when they heard of the aforementioned information, they called WWE headquarters and they denied that a decision has yet to be made.

Whether or not that is the company-answer until 7PM, when the rumored press conference occurs, remains to be seen.
