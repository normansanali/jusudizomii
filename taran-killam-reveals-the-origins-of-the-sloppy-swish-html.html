Taran Killam Reveals the Origins of the Sloppy Swish

“The move came first. And it was always just sort of a creepy, weird dance move to make people laugh… as I was writing it, I certainly realized that it wasn’t so far removed from a Macarena or Gangnam Style dance craze.”

-SNL cast member Taran Killam to HuffPo in an interview entirely centered around the “Sloppy Swish,” his silly dance from the sketch ”The Legend of Mokiki and the Sloppy Swish.” It’s the longest interview ever about a silly dance from a two-minute comedy sketch.
