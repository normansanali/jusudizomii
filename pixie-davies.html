Professional Life and Career

Firstly, Pixie is a child actress who made her debut with the BBC mini-series “The Secret of Crickley Hall” in 2012. Similarly, in 2014, she appeared as “Amanda” in the ‘Channel 4’ thriller drama action movie “Utopia”. The filmmakers also published this movie in Spanish version with the title “Aguas Rojas”. In the same year, she also appeared in “Nativity 3: Dude, Where’s My Donkey?” as “Pixie”.

She skyrocketed her popularity after playing the role of the peculiar child “Bronwyn Brantley” in the movie “Miss Peregrine’s Home for Peculiar Children”. Accordingly, she played the role of a young girl with superhuman strength in this movie.

Her most famous character was in the musical fantasy film “Mary Poppins Returns” in which she was featured as “Annabel” in 2018. Similarly, she is busy shooting for a period film “Our Man in America” which is based on a true story.
