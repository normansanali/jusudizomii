Joe Simpson, Tina Simpson Finalize Their Divorce

Papa Joe and Tina are officially done. After announcing their split and filing papers last October, Joe and Tina Simpson have finalized their divorce — ending their 34-year marriage — as of Tuesday, April 22, Us Weekly can confirm. The parents to Jessica and Ashlee Simpson (and grandparents to Bronx Wentz, 4, and Maxwell Johnson, 11 months) filed their papers in McLennan County Court, Texas. The court papers cited "discord or conflict of personalities" as the reason behind the split.

A rep for the famous family confirmed the split to Us in October, adding: "It is an amicable split and there is no third party involved. Any other related allegations are completely false."
