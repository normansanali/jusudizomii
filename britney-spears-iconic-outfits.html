Britney Spears' 10 most iconic outfits

Britney Spears is a woman of many talents. A bona fide hitmaker and pop culture legend, she is also a fashion icon, and has the archive to prove it. 

Coinciding with the release of her memoir, The Woman in Me, we've dipped into Britney's fashion history to revisit some of her most memorable looks.

 Her music video 'fits speak for themselves – from glossy red catsuits to air hostess mini dresses, Britney covered a lot of ground, using fashion to make a lasting impact on her audience.   

Don't Miss

But there's more where that came from - the star always brought her style A-game, be it on stage or the red carpet. Get ready for an influx of Y2K nostalgia…

Hello! Fashion shares Britney Spears' most iconic outfits:

Pretty in Pink  

A fresh-faced Britney brought the glam in sequin disc trousers and a classic tank top ahead of her win for the Single of the Year award with Baby One More Time.

 The Boob Tube Moment

Giving major Baby Spice vibes, Britney rocked a glossy pink boob tube while performing on tour. The jewel in the crown? Matching knee patches. Future fashion icon behaviour, if ever we saw it.

Fedora Fun

The Oops!... I Did It Again singer went for a seriously jaunty accessory, demonstrating her fondness for the unconventional fedora. The star teamed her hat with a vibrant tie-dye dress and a glitzy choker. If anyone can pull it off, it's Britney…

Futuristic Glam

Britney went hard on the metallics for a show at Wembley Arena, sporting a silver chainmail-leather one-shoulder jacket and bedazzled jeans.

 Hollywood glam meets Y2K

The star oozed Old Hollywood glamour at the Grammys at the turn of the millennium in a white cut-out gown with a fluffy stole.

 Super low-rise

Braving a seriously low-rise waistband, Britney exuded Y2K glam, teaming her tiered ruffle trousers with a distressed corset and a blingy choker.

 Double Denim

Britney and then-boyfriend Justin Timberlake can't possibly have predicted how iconic this matching denim moment would become, providing fashion fanatics with couple's costume inspiration for years to come.

 The Literal Boa

Britney took her performance to the next level in 2001 when she shared the stage with an albino python, recently recalling on Instagram how "scared" she felt when she was handed the reptile. Hardly surprising. Now that's true dedication.

 Pastel Perfection

The baker boy cap. The asymmetric ruching. The turned-up jeans. The skin-tight boots. So much to unpack here, but somehow Britney makes it work. Don't ask how. 

Crystal Extravaganza

The star wore a sheer mini dress in 2017, adorned with clusters of celestial embellishment over a sheer mesh, richly reminiscent of her iconic Toxic bodystocking.
