Cristiano Ronaldo's hotel-worthy Italian mansion with girlfriend Georgina and kids revealed

The legendary footballer owns an incredible property in Turin

    While he may be residing in the UK for his current club, the star also has a villa in Madeira and an enormous Italian mansion.
