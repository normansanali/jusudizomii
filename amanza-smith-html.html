Amanza Smith - Age, Family, Bio

Real estate agent who is best recognized for appearing on the second season of the Netflix reality show Selling Sunset. She and the other cast members all work for the Oppenheim Group. Her reality fame has earned her over 900,000 followers on her Instagram account. 

Before Fame

She has a degree in Interior design from Indiana State University. 

Trivia

She was a model and interior decorator before becoming a real estate agent. Before a reunion taping, she tested positive for COVID.

Family Life

She has two children with her former husband, NFL player Ralph Brown. She has previously dated actor Taye Diggs. 

Associated With

She and Meghan Markle have both appeared as a Deal or No Deal briefcase model.
