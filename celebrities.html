Mario Lopez News - Us Weekly

Name:Mario Lopez

Hometown:San Diego, CA

Birthday:October 10, 1973

About

Mario Lopez is most well-known for his role as A. C. Slater on the Saturday morning comedy Saved by the Bell.

After years out of the spotlight after the show’s cancellation, Lopez got a dose of reality on the series Dancing With the Stars. Lopez has also appeared in the TV series Pacific Blue, Nip/Tuck and the made for TV movie Husband for Hire.

Lopez was once married to actress Ali Landry, but the marriage was annulled a few weeks later due to Lopez’s alleged infidelity. The actor has also been linked to Dancing With the Stars contestant Karina Smirnoff.

The actor’s wife, former Broadway dancer Courtney Mazza, gave birth to their first child together, Gia Francesca, in September 2010. Son Dominic was born in 2013.
