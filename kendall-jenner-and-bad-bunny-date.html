Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny Enjoy Equestrian-Themed Date

Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny were spotted on an equestrian-themed outing at the Hidden Hills Equestrian Center on April 2, and it appears that they made the most of their time together in front of the cameras. The pair was photographed riding horses, with Jenner taking selfies while riding one horse together and watching Bad Bunny showcase his equestrian skills on another.

Sources say they spent around 90 minutes at the center, taking photos of each other and flaunting their cowboyin’ prowess. The couple has been linked romantically for several months, and these pictures come on the heels of sightings of them kissing in public and leaving events together. However, they have not officially announced their relationship to the public, making fans speculate whether they are an item.

Perhaps Jenner will introduce her new beau on the upcoming season of The Kardashians on Hulu, as it seems they have been spending a lot of time together lately. The rumors of a romance between Jenner and Bad Bunny had swelled since February when sources claimed to have seen them kissing at a club in Los Angeles.

The couple was later seen at the same restaurant as Justin and Hailey Bieber, leading tabloids to speculate that they were on a double date. The speculation continued when they were spotted displaying some very flirtatious body language leaving a sushi dinner in West Hollywood.

Images captured by TMZ showed them kissing passionately, with their bodies intertwined. They were also seen entering the same vehicle while leaving Jay-Z and Beyoncé’s Oscars after-party.

Their most recent outing saw them riding horses together in California, with Bad Bunny taking a vested interest in Jenner’s hobbies. They rode one horse together, with the Puerto Rican rapper holding on tight from behind, and took selfies while cuddled up on the horse.

Sources say they also watched each other riding separately during their 90-minute visit to the center. While their relationship status remains a mystery, one thing is clear: Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny seem to be having fun together.
