Triple H Takes Shot at the ECW Arena

While giving the WrestleMania AXXESS tour today, Triple H took a shot at the old ECW arena when they came across a display of what represented what ECW used to be. Triple H joked about how minor league ECW was by mentioning that you can play bingo just behind the curtain.

“What’s really cool is on the other side, you can sit down and play bingo,” said Triple H.
