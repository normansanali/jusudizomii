Bethenny Frankel has shared various looks over the years. Here are some of her most memorable.

Bethenny Frankel is an American reality television personality, entrepreneur, philanthropist and author. She entered the reality TV circuit as a contestant on the game show ‘The Apprentice: Martha Stewart’, in which she was the runner-up, in 2005. She rose to fame as a full-time cast member on the Bravo reality television series ‘The Real Housewives of New York City’, where she starred in season 1-3, and then seasons 7-11, before leaving the show for good.. Bethenny also received her own spin-off series ‘Bethenny Ever After’ (2010–12), ‘Bethenny & Fredrik’ (2018), and the talk show ‘Bethenny’ (2013–14).

Bethenny was previously married to Jason Hoppy. She gave birth to their daughter, Bryn in May of the same year. Bethenny and Jason separated in December 2012, and she filed for divorce in January 2013. Their divorce was finalized in July 2016. They went through a custody battle over their daughter.
