Fans Applauded The Way Taylor Swift Saved Dave Grohl At Paul McCartney's Party

Highlights

  • Dave Grohl recalls a hilarious moment at a celebrity party where Taylor Swift saved him from playing the piano while intoxicated.
  • Fans had a blast with the story and praised both Swift and Grohl for their quick thinking and talent.
  • Grohl still cherishes the memory of meeting his idol, Paul McCartney, and considers him the "baddest motherf***** in the world."

Thanks to Dave Grohl, fans got a snippet of what goes on behind the scenes at celebrity parties. As expected, the story was filled with twists and turns, involving Taylor Swift and the legend of The Beatles, Paul McCartney.

We'll take a look back at the story reveal alongside James Corden. Grohl had a blast at the party, but was put on the spot during a certain moment. We're going to reveal how Taylor Swift saved the day, and how the fans reacted to the iconic story.

Let's take a closer look.

Taylor Swift Stepped In To Sing A Song At Paul McCartney's Party After Dave Grohl Was Unsure About Playing The Piano

Speaking alongside James Corden, Dave Grohl made quite the reveal about a certain party. Fans had a blast with the story, as Grohl gave the details about his experience at Paul McCartney's party. As expected, it was an A-list guest list featuring the likes of Taylor Swift as well.

Grohl promised himself that he'd only stay until 10 PM, given that he had to drive his kids to the bus stop the next day. However, things took a turn once the party started. Things were getting exciting at 10 PM, so Grohl decided to stick around.

Things did take a bit of a turn when Grohl was asked to play the piano, or perform something. Grohl was very nervous given that he had one too many drinks. The artist recalls the experience, “He started playing this song and he’s blowing everyone’s mind. A new Paul McCartney song – weird! He finishes, and everyone looks at me, and they’re like, ‘Go on, Dave, play a song!’

“I’m like, ‘I don’t know how to play piano.’ I look around and all the guitars are left-handed. I’m screwed.” He added: “I’d maybe smoked a little pot – so I was already ‘challenged.’”

Thankfully, Taylor Swift completely saved Dave Grohl, asking to play something herself. Hilariously, she played one of Grohl's classics, but the artist just couldn't piece it together, “It’s like one of those nightmares where you’re running backwards, always late,” he said. Then it struck him: “I know this song. What the hell is this? And she was playing Best Of You. As if I weren’t high enough – that blew me into outer space! Taylor Swift, Best Of You, Paul McCartney… I lost my mind.”

Without a doubt, that's a moment Grohl won't ever forget, and he'll always be thankful for Swift sticking up for him and saving the day. The homage was a neat little bonus ultimately.

Fans Had A Blast With Dave Grohl's Interview Reveal Alongside James Corden

As expected, the news made headlines around the world. The moment on The Late Late Show with James Corden scored 3.5 million views on YouTube. For the most part, fans praised both Taylor Swift and Dave Grohl.

One fan wrote, "To me, it sounds like what Taylor did was pretty awesome. She saved his ass yet picked one of his songs so that he could still do what was asked of him. And she just happened to know one of his songs on piano? Wow. Pretty impressive."

Another fan appreciated the fact that Grohl was still conscious of his kids despite the moment with such A-list stars.

"Dave Grohl might just be the best man in the world. Leaves Paul Mcartney's party early because he has to get his kids to school on time."

A great moment for Grohl at the end of the day. In truth, Grohl still hasn't forgotten about the first time he met Paul McCartney.

Dave Grohl Still Has Memories Of The First Time He Met Paul McCartney

Everyone in the music scene has a tremendous amount of respect for Paul McCartney. Grohl said during his interview, “Just so you know, Paul McCartney is the baddest motherf***** in the world. He’s not only the coolest, he’s the best."

Dave Grohl still recalls the night he first met Paul McCartney. For Grohl, it was a career-changing moment getting to meet someone he looked up to.

“I noticed Paul McCartney out of the corner of my eye, chatting away with friends, and I couldn’t help but stare,” Grohl remembered. “There. He. Was."

“What happened next will forever remain a blur. I don’t recall exactly how Paul and I were introduced, what was said, or how long we talked, but I do remember putting on my best ‘this is not the most incredible thing ever to happen to me’ face while trying to keep from making a fool of myself.”

A great moment for Grohl and one he won't forget.
