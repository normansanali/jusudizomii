Leah Jenner Shares First Photo of Baby Daughter Eva

Look at that bundle of joy! Brandon Jenner‘s wife, Leah Jenner, shared the first-ever baby photo of her newborn daughter, Eva James Jenner, via Instagram on Wednesday, Sept. 23.

The snapshot features the first-time parent, 32, cradling her baby girl in bed. Little Eva, 2 months, is swaddled up in an apple-printed blanket and stares at the camera, her chubby cheeks on full display. Her enamored mother, meanwhile, gazes affectionately at her baby. The tot is the third biological grandchild of Caitlyn Jenner.

“It’s an honor, little one,” Leah captioned the pic. 

Leah and her husband, 33, welcomed their baby girl this past summer, which they announced via Instagram.

“On Wednesday, July 22nd I became a Mom,” Leah wrote at the time. “It has without a doubt been the most profoundly beautiful time in my life but also emotionally, spiritually and physically challenging too….and I am so grateful for every second of it. Brandon and I are taking care of our healthy, little baby girl, Eva James Jenner.”

Not one to forget her hot husband and fellow bandmate, Leah recently also posted a picture of herself cuddling in bed with Brandon.

“Thank you for your love..best 11 years of my life,” Don Felder’s daughter wrote, alongside the intimate shot of her hubby nuzzling her head. “Happy anniversary @brandontjenner #11yearstoday.”
