'Unexpected' Star Diego Reyes And Wife Shannon Welcome New Baby Boy!

Credit: TLC

Unexpected star Diego Reyes’ family just got even bigger! Diego and his wife Shannon Reyes welcomed their baby boy into the world. The couple announced they were expetcing back in June, and fans were shocked to see that Diego moved on from Emiley Noack so fast. 

In 2022, Unexpected star Diego Reyes‘ father dropped the news that he and Emiley Noack were no longer together. The ex-couple share daughter Aria Beckett.

“Wow. What a difference a week makes,” Diego’s father Fabian shared along with pictures of Diego and a new woman. “I went from having a houseful, all three boys, two daughter-in-laws, two granddaughters, an old dog, and a partridge in a pear tree. Football and school kicking off in a few weeks…. But today… the silence stinks, might have to turn on some Metallica.”

Soon after, Diego’s new wife confirmed the news.

“He stole my heart so I took his last name,” Diego’s wife, Shannon, shared along with a photo of her engagement ring on Facebook, according to Starcasm.

In June of 2023, the couple announced that they were starting a family together.

Diego showed off photos of him Shannon embracing as he held up a strip of ultrasounds. Shannon’s dress also revealed a growing bump.

Just four months after the announcement, the couple have officially welcomed their baby boy into the world!

Shannon shared sweet photos of their little one to Instagram.

The photos show their baby boy laying down on a bed in a light blue onesie with a matching hat. The newborn is sleeping in the first pic, and seemingly trying to awake in the second.
