Damian Priest to Address Attack on Bad Bunny During Mondays WWE RAW

WWE fans were shocked when Damian Priest attacked Bad Bunny on the April 3rd episode of Monday Night RAW.

The incident occurred after Bunny attacked Dominik Mysterio, leading Priest to defend his tag team partner. Despite not being a contracted WWE superstar, Bunny’s status has been a topic of interest for fans.

WWE reported that Bunny would not be revealing his medical status to the company. However, the story between the two is far from over.

During the latest episode of the WWE SmackDown LowDown, Priest revealed that he would address his issues with Bunny on the April 10 episode of Monday Night RAW.

The upcoming episode of Raw also includes matches such as WWE Women’s Tag Team Championships between Becky Lynch & Lita and Raquel Rodriguez & Liv Morgan, Riddle vs. The Miz, and Cody Rhodes addressing what’s next for him.

Although Bunny is not a WWE Superstar, he will host WWE Backlash in Puerto Rico. Fans can catch the live coverage of WWE RAW beginning Monday at 8 p.m. EDT on Ringside News.
