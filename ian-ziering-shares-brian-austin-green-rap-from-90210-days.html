Ian Ziering Shares Brian Austin Green Rap From 90210 Days

Talk about blushing! Brian Austin Green couldn’t help but get embarrassed when Ian Ziering brought up his hip-hop days during the Television Critics Association summer press tour. Shannen Doherty and her BH90210 costars stopped by Us Weekly’s video suite and Ziering, 55, revealed a very fun memory that includes Green’s musical days.

“Brian was very much into rap and hip-hop way back in the day. It’s still a big part of his life. He doesn’t like to admit it,” the Zombie Tidal Wave star told Us exclusively, while Green, 46, repeatedly asked him to stop.

“Remember when everyone had answering machines at home and you had a little remote to make it replay? Well I wanted to come up with a creative message and Brian helped me with it,” he revealed. He then asked Green to give him a beat but when he refused, Doherty provided one.
