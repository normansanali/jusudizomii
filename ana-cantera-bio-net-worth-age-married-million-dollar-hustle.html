Meet Million Dollar Hustle's Ana Cantera! Her Bio, Net Worth, Age, Married

Million Dollar Hustle premiered on Lifetime on 17 March 2022. Although viewers could watch the show also on FuboTV, Philo, and Sling.

The series follows Stormy Wellington, health and fitness influencer, author, entrepreneur, and self-made millionaire, and her elite group of leaders called the “Circle of Bosses”.

The cast members include Dianna Williams, Maniya Essence Canty, Nathalie Nicole Smith, Bawselady, Tammy Price, Bianca Shadai, and Ana Cantera.

Now, the rest of the writing is going to be about Ana Cantera only.

Ana Cantera On Million Dollar Hustle

Like Netflix’s famous Selling Sunset, Million Dollar Hustle highlights lifestyle marketing and a multi-billion-dollar industry dominated by women. These women, including Ana Cantera’s glamorous lifestyles, have been made by selling products, hosting events, and self-branding.

But since Ana and the mates were shown living so extravagantly, viewers have been curious about how legitimate the show is. However, the show, despite seeming over the top to some, also appeared to be legit so far.

Ana herself has been open over the years about working hard to make a name for herself. Her costars on the show also had their own individual stories of what brought them to where they are. And those stories also seemed to be real.

Million Dollar Hustle, so you know, was filmed primarily in Florida, specifically, in Miami.

Ana Cantera Net Worth

According to TV Guide Time, Ana Cantera had an estimated net worth of over $1 million as of March 2021. She is said to have been hustling since her early twenties.

Ana is now a network marketing professional. This happened when one day Ana realized that being an employee, having a traditional business at the same time, and working more than 12 hours a day, was not the life she wanted to live.

So, she was the age of 23 when she first met the Network Marketing profession. She immediately loved it and understood the concept of it.

In her first year, Ana built a network of more than 20,000 distributors and reached almost half a million dollars in commissions.

Three years ago from now, she joined her actual company where now she makes history as the first Latina on reaching the top rank position of the company.

Ana not just earned multiple 7 figures for herself but also helped other families in her team to become financially free. “Today, I do not depend on a salary, a job, or a boss; today I am the owner of my own business. I spend my time helping others to achieve their dreams”, Ana said about her success at TLC (Total Life Changes).

It was actually TLC’s executive ambassador Stormy Wellington who introduced Ana to TLC.

Ana was honored with the Grand Ambassador Rank with TLC in June last year. As part of the deal, she was offered increased weekly bonuses, a Branded Jeep Rubicon, and a custom diamond ring.
