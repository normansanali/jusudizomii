Sofia Carson Net Worth

Sofia Carson is a multi-talented entertainer who was born on April 10, 1993 in Fort Lauderdale, FL. She is best known for her starring role in the 2015 Disney Channel Original film Descendants, as well as her appearances in episodes of MTV's Faking It. She is also a vocalist and bilingual songwriter, having signed a recording contract with BMI in 2012.
