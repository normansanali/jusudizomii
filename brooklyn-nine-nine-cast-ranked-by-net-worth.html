Brooklyn Nine-Nine: The Cast Ranked By Net Worth

Brooklyn Nine-Nine first came out in 2013, and since then, it has become one of the most successful sitcoms of the decade. The first five seasons aired on FOX, but the company canceled the production of the series, and it seemed it would be over. Luckily, NBC picked it up, and they aired the sixth season in 2019 and the seventh in 2020.

The eighth and final season aired in September 2021 and ended September 16th, 2021. The series got a lot of recognition for the way they tackled important social issues, but let's not forget about the amazing cast.

10 Stephanie Beatriz - Detective Rosa Diaz

Net Worth: $2 Million

The actress that plays the talented yet terrifying detective Rosa Diaz is worth $2 million. Although Brooklyn Nine-Nine was her first major role and the series that gave her the fame she now has, she had been acting for a long time. She had a few minor roles in other TV series, such as The Closer, Southland, and Modern Family, and she's been in plays and independent films.

Since Brooklyn Nine-Nine, she's had a lot of new opportunities, including being a voiceover actor in BoJack Horseman.

9 Jason Mantzoukas - Adrian Pimento

Net Worth: $3 Million

Jason Mantzoukas played Adrian Pimento, a recurring character in the series who was once engaged to Rosa Diaz, and who spent over a decade undercover in the mafia. He's one of the funniest characters of the show, and the actor is worth $3 million, according to CelebrityNetWorth. Aside from Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Jason owes his net worth to his portrayal of Derek in The Good Place, The Tick Tock Man in John Wick: Chapter 3 - Parabellum, and his work as a stand-up comedian.

He also appeared in the movies Baby Mama, I Hate Valentine's Day, and The Dictator.

8 Dirk Blocker - Hitchcock

Net Worth: $4 Million

Dirk Blocker played the funny Hitchcock, and he's now worth $4 million. He is one of the members of the cast with the most extensive career. Coming from an artistic family, with an actor for a father and a producer for a brother, he has been dedicated to acting since the '70s.

He's been part of many TV series, such as Marcus Welby, M.D, M*A*S*H, and most recently, Criminal Minds, and films like Short Cuts and Mad City. His great work in Brooklyn Nine-Nine proves that he hasn't slowed down at all.

7 Melissa Fumero - Sergeant Amy Santiago

Net Worth: Between $2 Million - $4 Million

Melissa Fumero's net worth is reported to be $2 million acccording to sportskeeda, but CelebrityNetWorthreports her net worth as closer to $4 million. The actress that portrayed Sergeant Amy Santiago was already quite successful before getting a lead role in Brooklyn Nine-Nine. She was part of One Life to Live from 2004 to 2008 (where she met her husband, David Fumero), worked in independent films, and played a small role in Gossip Girl as one of Blair Waldorf's minions. She returned briefly to One Life to Live in 2010. Fumero also directed an episode of Brooklyn Nine-Nine's sixth season.

After Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Fumero appeared in Netflix's Blockbuster in 2022, but the sitcom only lasted for one season.

6 Joe Lo Truglio - Detective Charles Boyle

Net Worth: $5 Million

Detective Charles Boyle, Jake Peralta's best friend and the squad's chef, was portrayed by Joe Lo Truglio. The actor is worth $5 million. Before becoming one of the most loved characters in Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Joe starred as Billy on the TV series Horrible People in 2008. He was also the lead in Reno 911!, playing Deputy Frank Rizzo, and in the TV series Burning Love, playing Alex. He also worked in a lot of movies, including Pitch Perfect one and two, Gulliver's Travels, and About Last Night.

5 Andre Braugher - Captain Holt

Net Worth At Time Of Passing: $8 Million

Andre Braugher's work as Captain Holt was simply remarkable. He created a groundbreaking character that has become an icon of black, gay people in positions of power.

Braugher didn't just owe his net worth to Brooklyn Nine-Nine, though. He had very important roles before the show, including a notable role as Frank Pembleton on Homicide: Life on the Street, Owen Thoreau Jr. on Men of a Certain Age, and Thomas Searles in the movie Glory. Unfortunately, the talented actor passed away on December 12th, 2023 at the age of 61. Braugher will forever be remembered as an actor who portrayed two of TV's absolute best cops, according to Rolling Stone.

4 Chelsea Peretti - Gina Linetti

Net Worth: $20 Million

Chelsea Peretti, aka Gina Linetti, has a massive reported net worth of $20 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She was a regular character in Brooklyn Nine-Nine until season 6. After that, Chelsea appeared in one more episode. The cast said that they would have loved to have her on the show until the very end.

Aside from being an actress, she's a writer and has collaborated with Playgirl, The Village Voice, Details, and The Huffington Post online. Chelsea Peretti has been married to fellow comedian Jordan Peele since 2016.

3 Andy Samberg - Detective Jake Peralta

Net Worth: $25 Million

Andy Samberg was not only the lead in the series, starring as Detective Jake Peralta but also one of the producers. Right now, he's worth $25 million, according to CelebrityNetWorth. This impressive number is the product of years of hard work and dedication. He began his career in 2001 as part of a comedy trio called The Lonely Island. He started working on Saturday Night Live, making short comedy videos, and made a name for himself in show business. He also got to work for the 2005 MTV Movie Awards, and he's created the movie Popstar: Never Stop Never Stopping in 2016.

2 Terry Crews - Lieutenant Terry Jeffords

Net Worth: $25 Million

Terry Crews played Lieutenant Terry Jeffords in the show and has a net worth of $25 million. This makes sense when reviewing his impressive career and all the incredible roles he got to play. Before he became an important name in show business, he had a fairly successful career as an NFL defensive end and linebacker. Some of his most remarkable jobs as an actor aside from Brooklyn Nine-Nine are Julius Rock on the sitcom Everybody Hates Chris (2005 - 2009), the lead in Are We There Yet? from 2010 to 2013, and his work as a host in Who Wants to Be a Millionaire.

1 Kyra Sedgwick - Madeline Wuntch

Net Worth: $45 Million

Madeline Wuntch, Captain Holt's arch-nemesis, was portrayed by the great Kyra Sedgwick. Although she was not a lead in the show, she's definitely the wealthiest of the cast, with a net worth of $45 million. She earned her fortune with her great acting work, which she has dedicated herself to since she was a young girl. She got her first lead role when she was just 16 years old in the daytime soap Another World and hasn't slowed down since.
