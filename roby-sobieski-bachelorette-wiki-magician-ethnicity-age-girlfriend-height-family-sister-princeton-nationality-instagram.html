Roby Sobieski Biography – Roby Sobieski Wiki

Roby Sobieski (full name Robert Sobieski) is an actor, Magician, former child model, and reality television personality. He is from Los Angeles, California. Roby is known for his work on Birds of Prey (2020), Admission (2013), and Face (2012) as of his IMDb profile. He is the younger brother of Leelee Sobieski, an American artist, and retired actress. In July 2022, Roby will appear as a contestant in the 19th season of The Bachelorette. He is a magician at the World Famous Magic Castle. Roby graduated from Princeton University in Princeton, New Jersey.

According to his official website, Roby has used his mix of knowledge and magic to perform around the world, from the Great Wall of China to the Presidential Palace in Rwanda. He has also been hired to perform for prominent and renowned celebrities such as Steven Tyler from Aerosmith, Jay-Z, Snoop Dogg, Jerry Cantrell from Alice in Chains, Samuel L Jackson, Rosario Dawson, Facebook Co-founder Eduardo Saverin, the casts of The Young Mutants, Modern Family, and Stranger things.
