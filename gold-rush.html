Gold Rush: Latest episodes and cast member news updates

What was the latest season of Gold Rush?

Gold Rush season 13 aired in September 2022 with the newest series releasing in April 2023.

Do the Gold Rush cast members get paid?

Reports say cast members can get as much as $10,000 per episode to appear on the show, as well as the profits from any precious metals they find.

Who pays for the equipment on Gold Rush?

The main miners pay for the equipment, materials used and their crew while on the show. They keep whatever gold they mine, but if they make a loss, they have to cover that too.
