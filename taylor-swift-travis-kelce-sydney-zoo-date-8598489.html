Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce Had a Date at the Sydney Zoo

At long last, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are reunited again. It's been a week and four days since the couple were last publicly seen together at the Super Bowl, but after a long flight and traveling between time zones, they finally found their way back to each other in Sydney, Australia, where Swift is currently located for her Eras Tour. 

Their first stop? The zoo. On Thursday, Taylor and Travis were spotted taking a "private tour" at the Sydney Zoo. According to local media outlet 9News, the pair fed kangaroos, snapped selfies, and strolled around holding hands. For the casual outing, Taylor wore a red-striped tank top with spaghetti straps, denim shorts, and white sneakers and socks (see photos here), while Travis opted for a pair of patterned shorts, a black T-shirt, and a white baseball cap. 

Hours earlier, the NFL star touched down in Sydney via Swift's private jet, after teasing that he would "venture to an island real soon" on his New Heights podcast, adding that the best ones are "south" of America.

Ahead of Travis's arrival in Australia, Swift stepped out for a girls' night out with pal (and Eras Tour opener) Sabrina Carpenter at Pellegrino 2000 in Surry Hills. For the outing, the singer wore a black tank top with a bow and hook-and-eye detailing that she paired with a brown-and-black belted pleated miniskirt. She accessorized with a dainty gold bracelet and coordinating gold chain. Meanwhile, Sabrina opted for a red-and-white, polka-dot Bella Venice dress with eyelet lace trim.
