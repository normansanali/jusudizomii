Boomerang Energy (Twilight Masquerade 166)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Energy
Boomerang Energy
ブーメランエネルギー Boomerang Energy

Illus. Unknown

Boomerang Energy (Japanese: ブーメランエネルギー Boomerang Energy) is a Special Energy card. It is part of the Twilight Masquerade expansion.

Card text

All prints
As long as this card is attached to a Pokémon, it provides Energy.

If this card is discarded by an effect of an attack used by the Pokémon this card is attached to, attach this card from your discard pile to that Pokémon after attacking.

Trivia

This card's effect of reattaching itself from the discard pile after being discarded by the effect of an attack is similar to that of Burning Energy, first released in the BREAKthrough expansion.
