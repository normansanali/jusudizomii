Flashy Dunk in NBA 2K23

Flashy Dunk in NBA 2K23 are one of many different types of dunks that you can do in the game. In this guide, we are going to explain what the inputs for these dunks are. They’re kinda tricky to pull off, so you’ll still have to practice. However, once you know what to do, it should click more easily.

How to Do Flashy Dunks in NBA 2K23

To do Flashy Dunks in NBA 2K23, you have to go through a few steps fairly quickly. You need to press and hold the run button (R2 / right trigger) as you approach the hoop. Then, you have to flick the right stick up then down very fast and hold it. This should make your player do the one-handed Flashy Dunk. If you want to do a two-handed Flashy Dunk instead, then you need to flick the right stick up, and then up again and hold it. If you don’t get the timing right, you’ll probably end up with a regular dunk. So, I recommend you go and practice for a little while until you get it all down pat. It might take some time, so be patient.
